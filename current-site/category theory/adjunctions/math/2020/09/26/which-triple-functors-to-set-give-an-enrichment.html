When we have a Day convolution on presheaves on V,\calV,V, (I think we need to assume that V\calVV is small), then this is equivalent to the data of an enriching of C\calCC in presheaves on V\calVV with Day convolution.

All of this seems to come from the profunctor point of view, which seems to have the advantage that we donâ€™t have to assume that anything is representable. Solutions to existence problems always exist from this point of view, they are just presheaves rather than genuine objects.

It occurs to me that we could probably do this entire construction for a monoidal category, which seems like it should give the definition of a promonoidal category, or any other adjunction plus structure set up.
