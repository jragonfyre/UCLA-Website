Research

My current research interests are homotopy theory, algebraic geometry, and number theory.

Papers and Preprints

Triangulations of simplices with vanishing local h-polynomial

[arxiv] [pdf]

With André de Moura, Elijah Gunther, Sam Payne, and Alan Stapledon. Accepted for publication in Algebraic Combinatorics. From an undergraduate summer research project. We studied triangulations of simplices for which Richard Stanley’s local hhh-polynomial vanishes, and gave a recursive enumeration of all such subdivisions of the 3-simplex, Δ3.\Delta^3.Δ3.
